Hummus Burgers

This is a recipe for a pretty solid hamburger that I like to add hummus to. It's pretty good without it if you just want a good burger though.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patties
    • Garlic
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Olive oil
    • Any other seasoning you'll want on your patty
  • Hamburger Buns
    • Melted butter
    • probably some other stuff
  • toppings
    • Lettuce
    • Onions
    • Ketchup
    • Hummus
    • Cheese of your choice
    • Pickles
  • Probably some good hard cider (I like the cinnamon angry orchard)
  1. Combine your patty seasoning in the olive oil
  2. Dip each patty in the mixture
  3. Place each patty on the grill
  4. Add cheese to each patty as they cook
  5. Dip each bun in the melted butter (the white part only)
  6. Place each bun on grill next to patty to toast
  7. Pull everything off the grill
  8. Assemble your burger with the toppings you have selected
  9. Don't forget the Hummus!
