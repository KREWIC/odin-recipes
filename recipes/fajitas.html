Fajitas

These are the fajitas we usually make on these trips. We like to grill our fajitas so if you have one available that's a plus. Here are the ingredients and steps

  • Thin steaks
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheddar
  • Lettuce
  • tomatos
  • guacomole

After you've gathered your ingredients here are the steps to prepare and cook the food

  1. Cut your peppers and tomatos into strips
    • optional cut your thin steaks into strips
  2. Combine salt pepper garlic powder and any other seasoning you have into olive oil
  3. Dip thin steaks in the solution you have created
  4. leave on grill for 15-20 minutes or until thoroughly cooked
  5. Remove from grill and serve with tortillas and toppings
